📊 Parimutuel Betting Explained

CLUTCH uses parimutuel betting — all bets go into shared pools. Your odds depend on how much is bet on each outcome, not fixed by the platform.

Example: Brazil vs Argentina (Total Pool: 1,000 CLUTCH)
OutcomeAmount BetImplied Odds
Brazil Win500 CLUTCH50%
Draw300 CLUTCH30%
Argentina Win200 CLUTCH20%

The odds bar on each match card shows real-time pool distribution. It updates as more bets come in!
